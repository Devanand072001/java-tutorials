Access specifiers or modifiers:
Default:
	Anything declared in default is only visible inside the package.

Public:
	Anything declared in public can be accessed by any class inside the project folder. It can
be accessed by the classes in other packages.

Protected:
	Anything declared in protected is accessible only by the sub-class.

Private:
	Anything declared in private is accessible within the class. Even not accessible by the
subclass and instance of the class.

note:
	All modifiers can be access any where inside the project by the getters().
